What is Electronegativity?

Electronegativity is a measure of the tendency of an atom to attract a bonding pair of electrons. Florine is the most electronegativity.

02

What is a Empirical Formula?

Empirical formula is the formula of a compound expressed as the smallest possible whole-number ratio of subscripts of the elements in the formula.

03

What is a Molecular Formula?

Molecular formula is the formula of a compound in which the subscripts give the actual number of each element in the formula.

04

Why is Ammonia important?

Ammonia is important because it is a vital source of nitrogen to support plant life. Used to make dyes, plastic, explosives, drugs, nitric acid and ammonium hydroxide.
